About

The history of Hotel Arcabia begins with Mr. Jersen, who came to Ibiza with the vision of turning it into a piece of paradise. This is how he originally founded an inn, which after 30 years of operation turned into a top hotel.

Hotel Arcabia

Convenience, value, amenities and stellar staff are just a few of the reasons Ascaria Hotel guests return again and again. Our fantastic location offers great views and fabulous access to our pool and all the attractions such as our home cinema and top restaurant. Our perfectly prepared rooms, friendly staff are complemented by a long list of amenities and services that our hotel offers to customers. This makes the Ascaria hotel one of the best hotels in Ibiza.
